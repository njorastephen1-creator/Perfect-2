Care Group Teacher Guide

1. Getting Started

  • Click Create Account to register
  • Use your email & a secure password
  • Add your name, bio, and optional photo

2. Managing Students

  • Click Assign Student → Search main system by name/class/phone
  • Tap + Assign to add them to your group
  • Click 🗑️ to remove from your list (doesn't delete from main system)

3. Attendance & Tracking

  • Click Take Attendance to mark daily presence
  • Use calendar icon 📅 on student cards for 30-day history
  • Message absentees instantly via WhatsApp

4. Export & Share

  • Click Export PDF to download a formatted report with attendance
  • All student progress data is pulled live from the main system
